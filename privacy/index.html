Privacy notice

How the waitlist data is used.

This privacy page covers the information collected through the waitlist form and the basic handling expectations for outreach and follow-up.

What we collect

We collect the email address you submit through the waitlist form and any optional interest categories you select.

We do not ask for payment information or highly sensitive personal information through this website.

How we use it

  • To contact you about ForAll Sense updates and launch information.
  • To understand whether inbound interest is coming from pharma, government, NGO, or research audiences.
  • To reply to inbound requests related to evidence review, pilots, or partnership conversations.

How the form is processed

The waitlist is processed through a hosted web-form workflow used to collect inquiries and launch interest.

If the form cannot be submitted successfully, the page falls back to the contact email shown on the homepage and in the footer.

Retention and contact

Waitlist information should be retained only as long as it is useful for launch updates and follow-up conversations.

To request removal or ask a question about submitted information, contact contact@foralltech.in.
